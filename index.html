Projects

The Milgram Experiment

March-August 2025

The Milgram Experiment pushes the boundaries on what a game can be. It puts the player through the famous Milgram Experiment and tests how they would react in the face of pleading and helpless citizens. Players take the role of a Patriot Test administrator in a fictional country. NPCs come in and take the test, with the player being instructed to “administer a motivational stimulus” in response to any incorrect answers. The player must decide whether they will submit to authority and cruelly execute innocent civilians, or rebel and potentially save lives. For this project I was the team lead as well as the lead designer. I was in charge of the majority of design decisions and with keeping the project on track. Currently a demo is available with one encounter, although more will be released in the coming months.

Gunslinger Breakout

April–August 2024

Gunslinger Breakout is an unfinished 3D FPS puzzle game where the player must use a revolver full of bullets with special abilities in order to solve puzzles. The player finds themselves in an abandoned facility and must solve increasingly complex puzzles in order to escape. There are 6 different bullet types available to the player, Cryo, Ricochet, Airburst, Power Charge, Gravity, and Persist. Use any combination of six of these bullets to solve puzzles and escape the facility! For this project I was a gameplay and level designer, implementing and designing all levels. Grand Prix

September–December 2024

Grand Prix is a fast-paced strategic card game where players need to strike at the perfect moment to secure the victory. Players must use their cards to beat everyone in consecutive 1v1 races, spending resources they have gained strategically to ensure victory. Grand Prix combines bluffing aspects of games like Coup and Liar’s dice with the timeless classic War. I was the team lead and lead designer for this card game. I really wanted to create a game where players had some incentive to “punt” or cut their losses since I believe this is a very under used game mechanic.

Time Shift

January–April 2024

Time shift is a 3D first person puzzle game where the player must travel through time in order to solve puzzles and escape the facility! This game was created as a part of a group project at my previous University, UC Irvine. In this game the player has the ability to travel through time to the future and back to the present. The facility they are in looks different depending on what time they are in, and they must use this to solve puzzles and escape.

Spacial Defense

August–June 2023

Spacial Defense is a 2.5D Tower Defense game where the player must manage 3 towers with different abilities to prevent waves of enemies from reaching their base. Move towers alongside the enemies in order to beat waves. After certain score milestones players can upgrade their towers. Compete to get a highscore in this arcade style game! I created everything for this game during highscool. I designed and programmed everything in Unity.
