EX-Traterrestrial

EX-Traterrestrial was the winning submission to the 2025 Uniting the Valley AZ Game Cup. Over two days, my team and I built the entire game and competed against 9 other teams from 5 universities.

As team lead, I delegated tasks, established the core loop, maintained a unified vision, and ensured zero merge conflicts during rapid development. I also pitched the game to the judges, helping our team secure the win.
