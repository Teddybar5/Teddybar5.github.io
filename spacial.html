Spacial Defense

Spacial Defense was a solo project created with Unity that I released in 2021. My Duties Included:

  • Used a Queue to move towers throughout the level
  • Utilizing Coroutines to spawn different types of enemies at specific intervals
  • Creating a Tower Upgrade system based on points
  • Implemented a Trackable High Score
  • Creating the three tower (bomb, speed, freeze) art assets in MagicaVoxel
  • Creating the enemy art assets
  • Creating the music for the game
  • Designing the game space and both levels
  • Designing the UI and Tutorial Level
  • All other assets belong to their repsective owners
