The Milgram Experiment

The Milgram Experiment explores the boundaries of what a game can be, immersing players in a reimagining of the famous psychological study. Players step into the role of a Patriot Test administrator in a fictional country, tasked with “administering a motivational stimulus” whenever NPCs answer incorrectly.

I served as both team lead and lead designer, overseeing the core vision, directing design decisions, and guiding the team through development. While currently available as a one-encounter demo, the game is in active development with additional scenarios planned.
