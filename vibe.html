Whats The Vibe?

Whats The Vibe? was a group project done at my university, UCI. I was the lead programmer and game esigner.My Duties Included:

  • Coded and Developed the Buildings and their functionality
  • Created the AI player from scratch that operates with their best interest in mind and always makes a move or multiple if possible
  • Added and Animated the helper guy
  • Conceptualized the idea for the game, Competitive city builder
  • Created and added functionality to all of the UI throughout the game
  • Helped with the event system
